WHY FLUX?

My website, Flux, is like my personal song library, where I store chords and lyrics of my favorite songs that I learn and sing on my guitar. I've been playing guitar for a year now, and it's something I really enjoy. I used to jot down chords and lyrics in a notebook, but I had the idea to digitize this process.

The design of Flux was clear in my mind from the start. As an Informatics student with few creative projects, I wanted to fully immerse myself in this one. I decided to create the layout in Illustrator, ensuring a diverse mobile version for the homepage. I'm thrilled with how it turned out, just as I envisioned, even if it meant dealing with 1000 lines of CSS :D
